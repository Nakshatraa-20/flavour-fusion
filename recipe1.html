Italian Pasta 🍝

Prep Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Veg

Ingredients

  • Pasta (250g)
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Parmesan cheese (to taste)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Red chilli flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve ½ cup pasta water before draining.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic until golden and fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper and chilli flakes. Simmer for 8–10 mins.
  4. Add drained pasta to the sauce. Toss well, adding pasta water if needed.
  5. Serve hot topped with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

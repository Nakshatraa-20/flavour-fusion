Chocolate Cake 🎂

Prep Time: 50 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Dessert

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (1.5 cups)
  • Cocoa powder (½ cup)
  • Sugar (1.5 cups)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Butter (½ cup, melted)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Baking powder (1.5 tsp)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • Dark chocolate (for ganache)
  • Heavy cream (½ cup)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, melted butter, milk, and vanilla. Mix until smooth batter forms.
  4. Pour batter into pans and bake for 30–35 mins. Test with a toothpick.
  5. For ganache: heat cream and pour over chopped chocolate. Stir until silky.
  6. Cool cakes completely, then frost with ganache. Slice and serve!

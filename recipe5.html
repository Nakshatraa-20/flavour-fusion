Tomato Soup 🍅

Prep Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Quick Meal

Ingredients

  • Ripe tomatoes (6 large)
  • Onion (1, chopped)
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Vegetable broth (2 cups)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • Heavy cream (¼ cup)
  • Tomato paste (1 tbsp)
  • Sugar (1 tsp)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook for 10 mins until tomatoes break down.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth, add sugar, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 mins. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  5. Blend the soup until completely smooth using a hand blender or stand blender.
  6. Stir in heavy cream, reheat gently, garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Healthy Salad 🥗

Prep Time: 15 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Veg

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce (2 cups)
  • Cherry tomatoes (½ cup, halved)
  • Cucumber (1, sliced)
  • Red onion (¼, thinly sliced)
  • Bell peppers (½, diced)
  • Avocado (1, diced)
  • Feta cheese (¼ cup, crumbled)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Lemon juice (1 tbsp)
  • Salt, pepper & herbs to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Tear lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and bell peppers.
  4. Gently fold in diced avocado and crumbled feta cheese.
  5. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper and herbs for dressing.
  6. Drizzle dressing over salad, toss gently and serve immediately.

Hyderabadi Biryani 🍛

Prep Time: 60 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Category: Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice (2 cups)
  • Chicken (500g)
  • Yogurt (1 cup)
  • Onions (2, fried golden)
  • Saffron (a pinch in warm milk)
  • Biryani masala (2 tbsp)
  • Ginger-garlic paste (1 tbsp)
  • Mint & coriander leaves
  • Ghee (3 tbsp)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, ginger-garlic paste and salt for 30 mins.
  2. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook marinated chicken in a heavy pot until half done.
  4. Layer rice over the chicken. Top with fried onions, mint, saffron milk and ghee.
  5. Seal the pot with a tight lid and cook on dum (low heat) for 25 mins.
  6. Gently mix before serving. Serve hot with raita and mirchi ka salan.
